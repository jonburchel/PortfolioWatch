# PortfolioWatch Features

PortfolioWatch is designed to be a comprehensive yet unobtrusive tool for monitoring your financial portfolio and the broader market. Below is a detailed breakdown of its capabilities.

## üìà Market Monitoring

*   **Real-time Updates:** Stock prices and market data are automatically refreshed every 60 seconds during market hours.
*   **Market Indexes:** Optional visibility for major market indexes (e.g., S&P 500, NASDAQ, Dow Jones) to keep a pulse on the overall market direction.
*   **Intraday Sparklines:** Visual representation of price action throughout the day for individual stocks and your total portfolio.
*   **Earnings Tracking:** Automatically checks for and displays upcoming earnings dates for your watched stocks (updates every 4 hours).

## üíº Portfolio Management

*   **Portfolio Mode:** Toggle between a simple watchlist and a detailed portfolio view.
*   **Holdings Tracking:** Enter the number of shares you own for each stock to calculate:
    *   **Market Value:** The current total value of your position.
    *   **Day Change ($):** How much your position has gained or lost today in dollars.
    *   **Portfolio Weight:** Visual indicators showing what percentage of your portfolio each stock represents.
*   **Total Portfolio Performance:** Real-time calculation of your total portfolio value, total daily gain/loss, and overall daily percentage change.

## üõ†Ô∏è Customization & Settings

*   **Theming:** Choose between **Light**, **Dark**, or **System** themes to match your desktop environment.
*   **Window Customization:**
    *   **Opacity Control:** Adjust the transparency of the window to blend into your desktop.
    *   **Custom Title:** Rename the window to whatever you prefer (e.g., "My Retirement", "Tech Stocks").
    *   **Always on Top:** Option to keep the widget visible above other windows.
*   **Sorting:** Sort your list by:
    *   Symbol
    *   Name
    *   Price Change (%)
    *   Day Change ($) (Portfolio Mode only)
    *   Market Value (Portfolio Mode only)
*   **Startup:** Option to automatically launch PortfolioWatch when Windows starts.

## üíæ Data Management

*   **Import/Export:** Easily backup your watchlist and portfolio data to a JSON file, or transfer your setup to another computer.
*   **Reset:** "Factory reset" option to clear all data and settings if you want to start fresh.

## üîç Search & Navigation

*   **Smart Search:** Quickly find and add stocks by typing their symbol or company name.
*   **Quick Links:**
    *   Click through on a stock to open details directly in Google Finance.
    *   Quick access to the GitHub repository for updates or to report issues.
